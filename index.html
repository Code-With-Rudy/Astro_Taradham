About East Indian Astrology

Ancient Wisdom, Modern Guidance

East Indian Astrology, also known as Vedic Astrology or Jyotish, is an ancient system of astrology that originated in India over 5,000 years ago. This sacred science uses precise astronomical calculations and time-tested techniques to provide deep insights into your life's journey.

Unlike Western astrology, Vedic astrology uses the sidereal zodiac system, which accounts for the actual positions of celestial bodies. This provides more accurate predictions and profound spiritual guidance for your personal and professional life.

Our consultations cover birth chart analysis, planetary transits, dasha periods, compatibility matching, career guidance, health predictions, and remedial measures to harmonize planetary influences.
